# Morning Enrichment

Read `workos-state/priorities.md` and `workos-state/vectors.md` from the vault.

## What to do

After the regular daily planning output, append a **Career Lens** section that connects today's work to career vectors.

1. **Map today's priorities to vectors** — Which of today's todos touch which vectors?
2. **Staleness alerts** — Any vectors with no evidence in 14+ days? Suggest a small action today
3. **Coaching nudges** — If coaching observations from `workos-state/coaching.md` relate to today's work, surface them as gentle reminders
4. **Weekly plan alignment** — Check `workos-state/weekly-plan.md` and note if today's focus aligns with the week's Top 3

## Output format

Append this to the daily plan:

```
---

## Career Lens

**Vectors in play today:** [Vector A], [Vector B]

**Stale vectors:** [Vector C] (no evidence in 18 days) — consider [small action]

**Coaching reminder:** [Observation topic] has been surfacing for [N] days. Today's [todo/meeting] is a chance to address it.

**Week alignment:** Today's focus hits 2 of 3 Top 3 items. [Item X] hasn't been touched yet this week.
```

## Rules

- Keep it to 4-6 lines max — this is a quick lens, not a career counseling session
- Only surface coaching reminders at escalation level 3+
- Skip sections that have nothing to report (don't say "no stale vectors")
